CHECK 3 — AMFI API CODE VALIDATION (mfapi.in)
----------------------------------------------------------------
The scheme codes provided in the capstone documentation were
tested against the live mfapi.in API. 5 out of 6 codes returned
incorrect fund names.

Code    Expected                  Actual                         Status
------  ------------------------  ----------------------------  ---------
125497  HDFC Top 100 Direct       SBI Small Cap Fund Direct      WRONG
119551  SBI Bluechip Direct       Aditya Birla PSU Debt Fund     WRONG
120503  ICICI Bluechip Direct     Axis ELSS Tax Saver Fund       WRONG
118632  Nippon Large Cap Direct   Nippon India Large Cap Direct  CORRECT
119092  Axis Bluechip Direct      HDFC Money Market Fund         WRONG
120841  Kotak Bluechip Direct     Quant Mid Cap Fund Direct      WRONG

Root Cause : SEBI's 2018 mutual fund recategorisation circular
             caused AMCs to rename schemes. AMFI codes in the
             documentation are outdated.

Corrected codes verified and used:
  119598 — SBI Large Cap Fund Direct Growth
  120586 — ICICI Prudential Large Cap Fund Direct Growth
  120465 — Axis Large Cap Fund Direct Growth
  118632 — Nippon India Large Cap Fund Direct Growth
  125497 — SBI Small Cap Fund Direct Growth (substitute)

Note: Correct code for Kotak Large Cap Fund (formerly Kotak
Bluechip) could not be confirmed via mfapi.in registry.
Result   : FAIL (documentation codes) / PASS (corrected codes)
